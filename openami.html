OpenAMI

Hardware thread within ISV Meter Interoperability · EnAccess · Energy IoT · NESL

OpenAMI is driving actionable energy telemetry — per-customer reads, credit, tamper, and disconnect semantics operators can bill and act on, not utility-scale archive data alone. Leakage visibility at every part of the system — feeder, branch, and customer — so operators can locate loss and theft, not just see aggregate feeder imbalance. Affordable open stack for mini-grids: vendor-agnostic gateways, MQTT northbound interop, geo asset topology, and operator backends.

Community · Discord

OpenAMI discussions live on the OSEA (Open Source Energy Access) Discord server managed by EnAccess. Join: discord.osea-community.org · how to join ↗. ISV Tech Comm goal: participate more actively — introduce work, answer integration questions, share wiki artifacts.

Program overview: enaccess.org/openami. The MeshEMS board is aligned hardware for learning that stack in the field — multi-tenant edge metering at the upstream/downstream boundary, documented openly for community operators.
