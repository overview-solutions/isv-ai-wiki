OSEAS is the global symposium for open-source innovation in energy access — co-organized by EnAccess and the WRI Polsky Center, with Carnegie Mellon University Africa as the 2026 hosting partner. The 2026 program adds expanded working groups, a product fair, donor roundtable, site visit, and an improved hackathon.

Same week in Kigali

EventDatesVenue / notes
OSEAS 2026 26–27 Oct 2026 Open Source in Energy Access Symposium — hackathon, demos, working groups
Global Off-Grid Solar Forum & Expo 27–29 Oct 2026 Kigali Convention Centre — industry expo & policy forum

The two events run in parallel in the same week and venue ecosystem. ISV Tech Comm attendance can span both — open-stack builders at OSEAS and minigrid / DRE operators at the Forum.

ISV @ OSEAS hackathon

IEEE Smart Village will contribute a code challenge to the OSEAS 2026 hackathon. Challenge brief, judging criteria, and starter repos are in planning — aligned with ISV’s OpenAMI and MeshEMS / Street-EMS stacks (multi-tenant metering, northbound interoperability, village-scale EMS).

Expect themes around open tools that help in-country teams deploy, operate, and sustain community electrification — not slide decks. Details will be posted here and coordinated with EnAccess as the symposium program firms up.
