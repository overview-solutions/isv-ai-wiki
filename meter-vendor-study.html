Key findings

  • Donsun + SteamaCo link verified: SteamaCo’s product page states Savi meters are “provided by Donsun Shenzhen” [6] — same hardware family ISV is evaluating.
  • DLMS + STS often co-exist on West Africa / China utility meters (Donsun, Inhemeter, Calin, Mojec) per datasheets — but key hierarchies differ from OEM stacks (see Tech Comm topology notes).
  • SparkMeter is a proprietary mesh + ThunderCloud stack in public materials; no DLMS interoperability documentation found [9][13].
  • IEEE 2030.5 appears on grid-tied DER gateways (Enphase utility client) — not on village prepaid meters. Do not score 2030.5 at the meter row.
  • SunSpec is documented for Enphase SunSpec-700 Modbus and Victron’s integration with SunSpec inverters — not for Donsun/Inhemeter revenue meters.
